ABOUT COIN

Coin is an innovative and secured app that allows you to make payments through your mobile devices.

It replaces cash everywhere! Home delivery, Restaurants, Shopping, you name it ...

HOW TO USE COIN

  • 1. Detect the merchant

    Just hit the Scan button and our app will identify the merchant via a bluetooth beacon

  • 2. Confirm billing details

    Enter the payment amount and make an express check-out to payment gateway

  • 3. Make Payment, Get Discounts

    Make payment using Credit/Debit cards or net banking and get discount coupons for the purchase you just made.

FAQ

  • How do I select the merchant to pay?

    All you need to do is hit the ‘Scan for merchant’ button and our app will identify the merchant to be paid using Bluetooth technology. If the merchant is one of your regulars, you can simply mark it as ‘Favourite’ and we will remember its details for the next time.

  • Do I need to have a prepaid wallet?

    No, that’s the Coin advantage. Pay after you receive your order and get reward ‘Coins’. As you accumulate enough Coins, you can use them to make payments. No need to lock-in your money beforehand.

  • How do I get the discount coupons?

    For every purchase made via Coin, you are entitled to discount coupons equal in value to your transaction amount. You can keep track of your purchases and get the discount coupons at a convenient time, no need to hurry there.

  • What if a transaction fails?

    We assure you that won’t happen. Even so, in case the money debited from your account doesn’t reach the merchant, we would refund the whole amount back to your Coin wallet, which you can use to pay the next time you use Coin.
